Formula:

G = R * (20 + C - 19 * K) * M - 500
where R is a variable with the formula int(((T - D) / 2)^2) - 4 * I, altogether capped out at 999

G = Gold in holds
M = Mood (0=mutinous, 1=unhappy, 2=content, 3=happy, 4=very happy)
T = Time in months at sea since last time plunder was divided
D = Difficulty level (4=Apprentice, 3=journeyman, 2=adventurer, 1=rogue, 0=swashbuckler)
I = Items boosting morale (0=none, 1=3 stringed fiddle, 2=concertina)
C = Crewsize
K = Cook specialist (0=none, 1=have)
